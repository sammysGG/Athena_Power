2. Process Overview

Cascade CF-301 consists of three centrifuges in series (A → B → C) sharing a coolant header, a feed header from FCV-101, and a discharge manifold to the product tank LT-102. The PLC is a generic Modbus TCP slave; the SCADA bridge polls it once per second and persists telemetry to a local SQLite store.

3. Modbus Register Map

5. Trip Recovery

If XA-301 latches, allow the bowl to coast below 400 RPM and chamber temperatures below 58 °C / 62 °C before requesting Reset from the Overview tab. The cascade re-enters AUTO GOVERNOR on the next scan after reset.
